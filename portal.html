Welcome to the Sunny Preps Portal

Our portal is designed to keep tutors, students and parents connected. Tutors can post homework, assignments and tests; students can view and complete their tasks; and parents can monitor progress and see what their child is learning. We currently manage assignments and communication through Slack and other third‑party tools, but this portal provides a single place to view the latest information.

Curriculum Overview

The Digital SAT Math section is divided into two modules and focuses on the areas of math that play the biggest role in college and career success【25276288267937†screenshot】. You will master:

  • Algebra: Linear equations, inequalities, and functions.
  • Advanced Math: Nonlinear expressions, quadratic and exponential functions, rational functions, and manipulating expressions.
  • Problem Solving & Data Analysis: Ratios, rates, percentages, descriptive statistics, and analyzing tables and graphs.
  • Geometry & Trigonometry: Lines, angles, circles, triangles, area/volume, right triangle trigonometry, and radian measure.

Approximately 13–15 questions test Algebra and Advanced Math, and 5–7 questions each test Problem Solving & Data Analysis and Geometry & Trigonometry【206288302703049†screenshot】.

Course Details

This asynchronous, self-paced digital course delivers new learning material and practice problems to you each day via our Slack workspace. You’ll receive concise explanations, targeted practice questions and step-by-step solutions, but you’re responsible for working through the material on your own schedule. Assignments are graded automatically and progress is tracked, however there is no direct feedback from an instructor.

  • Topics include linear and quadratic equations, exponential functions, rational expressions, systems of equations, ratios and proportions, descriptive statistics, probability, geometry (circles, triangles, polygons), trigonometric ratios and more.
  • Daily lessons and practice sets are posted to Slack. You decide when to study each subtopic and how quickly to move through the material.
  • Assignments are automatically graded and your progress is tracked, but you won’t receive personalised instructor feedback.
  • The adaptive question generator adjusts to your performance, providing fresh practice questions so you never run out.
  • All materials are unique to this course and cannot be found anywhere else.
